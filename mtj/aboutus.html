About Melanie Taylor Jefferson

Melanie Taylor Jefferson is an attorney that has been a solo practitionerin the legal eld for almost thirty (30) years. She graduated from Rhodes College with a Bachelor of Arts in English and a Juris Doctorate Degree from Cecil C. Humphries School of Law at the University of Memphis.

Her current primary areas of practice are Probate and Mediation. However, she initially served as a Judicial Law Clerk under Chancellor Lewis and Chancellor Floyd Peete, Jr. in Part II of Chancery Court.

Attorney Taylor Jefferson currently practices law full time and is employed part-time with the Shelby County Government as a Divorce Referee.

Attorney Taylor Jefferson enjoys practicing law because it gives her theability to help people navigate the judicial system with a Christian attorney that will do her best to help them resolve their problem and understand the system.

Not only does Attorney Taylor Jefferson help people solve legal problems, she serves as a mentor to anyone seeking help and consults with various attorneys in the Memphis area to brainstorm eective solutions to issues within their cases.

Attorney Taylor Jefferson is a member of the Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority, Incorporated, Pi Chi Omega Chapter in West Memphis and serves onthe Board of Directors for the Young Actors Guild. Her past and current professional a8liations include membership in the American Inns of Court, Memphis Bar Association, Association of Women Attorneys, and the Ben F. Jones Chapter of the National Bar Association.

Attorney Taylor Jefferson is married to Jeremy Jefferson; has a stepson named Jacob and two (2) dogs, Molly and Prince.Her hobbies include working out, reading, dancing, enjoying her family and the company of good friends.
