About Melanie E. Taylor Jeffers

Melanie E. Taylor Jefferson is an attorney that has been a solopractitioner in the legal field for almost thirty (30) years.

Her current primary areas of practice are Probate and Mediation. Attorney Taylor currently practices law full time and is employed part-time with the Shelby County Government as a Divorce Referee.
