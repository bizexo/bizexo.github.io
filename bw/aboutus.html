COMPANY OVERVIEW

Barbour Waste Removal, LLC., provides residential/commercial trash removal and recycling services for the Washington and Maryland metropolitan areas. We are a privately owned and operated waste removal company located in Oxon Hill, Maryland.

Our philosophy is “best of the best.” Yet, at the same time, having concerns for the needs of our customers and employees. Therefore, there are no shortcuts to excellent customer service. That is what Barbour Waste Removal will provide to all of its customers at all times.

Barbour Waste Removal business model

  • Each operated truck carries a minimum of one-million-dollar liability insurance.
  • The company does not hire transient workers.

Certification

  • MBE certified with Maryland Department of Transportation (MDOT)
  • Prince Georges County Based Business, Washington Suburban Sanitary Commission (WSSC)
  • Washington Metropolitan Area Transit Authority (WMATA) for trash removal services.
